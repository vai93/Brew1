About Us

Welcome to my cafe

At Brew, we believe that a cup of coffee can brighten your day and bring people together. Located in the heart of Baroda, Brew is a place where you can escape the hustle and bustle, enjoy a warm atmosphere, and savor the finest coffee and treats.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Brew started as a dream to create a space where the love for coffee and community could flourish. Our founders, vaibhavi, have always been passionate about crafting the perfect cup and providing a welcoming environment for everyone. With a focus on quality and sustainability, Brew quickly became a beloved spot for coffee enthusiasts and casual visitors alike.
